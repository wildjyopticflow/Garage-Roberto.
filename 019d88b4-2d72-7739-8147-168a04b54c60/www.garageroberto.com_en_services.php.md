**Programming and diagnostics**

As technology evolves rapidly in the automotive field, our technicians constantly receive training to be able to respond to any change in motor vehicles. We specialize in electrical problems. Using our analyzers and oscilloscope, we will be able to find even the most complex ones which are sometimes thought to be impossible to find problems.

**Air conditioning**

It should be noted that it is very important that the air conditioning filling and checking for leaks are performed in compliance with the environmental regulations of Quebec. Moreover, our specialists are technically certified.

![](http://www.garageroberto.com/images/r12.png)

**Alignment**

Did you know that after every change in a suspension part, you should align the wheels. This allows for greater safety and handling on the road and even tire wear. 3D technique used by our experts, provides more accurate performance.
